How We Do Differently

With tedious and routine transactional processes and compliance work properly managed, our NovaAnalysts work closer with you in analyzing, benchmarking and reporting the financial and management information underlying your business operations to help you understand better your business performance; while our NovaAdvisors, who act as your CFO and trusted advisors, will regularly meet and assist you in formulating the business strategies, setting targets, assess business performance, monitor trends and identify opportunities for performance improvement and value creation.

Cloud Based Accounting

Nowadays, cloud technology allows the process of accounting and tax compliance becoming much more efficient and effective. By partnering with MEGI, a Cloud-based Accounting Software which is designed for China, our well trained Cloud Accountants have made the complicated and tedious compliance processes more simpler and straightforward; more importantly, make your life easier. You can always access and view your dashboard anytime and anywhere so that you won’t miss any important payments from and to your important customers and vendors respectively. More than that, you can timely keep track of your business performance and operational targets.
